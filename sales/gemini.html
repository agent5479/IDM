Unmatched Advantage: Why Choose IDM?

IDM screens are designed for true operational efficiency. Unlike cumbersome, fixed-site machinery, our screens feature a tow-ready design. They can be easily hitched to a tractor or suitable vehicle for immediate and effortless relocation across your site or between jobs. This eliminates costly transport logistics and downtime, ensuring you can screen material exactly where you need it, instantly.

Our equipment is built rugged, heavy-duty, and engineered for the harshest conditions. IDM screens handle high-impact, high-volume material with superior structural integrity, leading to significantly lower wear-and-tear and minimal maintenance requirements. This robust construction delivers a longer service life and higher uptime than standard alternatives.

IDM models offer complete flexibility. While excelling at heavy pre-screening, our screens are also capable of processing fine-grade materials, giving you the ability to produce a wider range of high-value, marketable products—from clean fill and road base to landscaping soil. The 45° adjustable deck slope allows for precise control over material flow and separation.
