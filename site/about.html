About Site Machinery

Who We Are

Site Machinery is a specialist supplier of smaller scale earthmoving and soils and gravel processing equipment. We are proud to be the exclusive New Zealand supplier of DeSite Proscreen equipment.

The unique vibratory screening system on the Pro-screens is only available on machines designed and manufactured by DeSite and supplied in New Zealand by Site Machinery Ltd.

Sister Company: Visit our sister company DeSite Products Australia for additional product information, videos, and resources.

Our Products

We currently have a range of soil screeners at very reasonable prices. Our equipment range includes:

  • Proscreen SLG-108VFRB - Heavy duty screener for large operations
  • Proscreen SLG-78VF - Popular portable screener with multi-slope deck
  • Proscreen SLG-78VF with Flow Control - Enhanced version with flow control
  • Proscreen SLG-68V - Compact screener for smaller operations
  • Telehandler Bins - Strong, durable bins for construction sites
