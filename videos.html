Proscreen SLG-108VFRB

The DeSite SLG 108VFRB is a highly portable vibratory screener for sand, soil, rock, compost, wood debris, landscape and construction debris and more.

It can produce a large amount of quality usable material in a short amount of time.

Proscreen SLG-78VF

DeSite SLG 78VF-5 Topsoil Screener introduced in 2017. The screen deck is 80" x 52". It comes with a 3600 RPM Vibratory Pack, Cantilevered Screen Deck Suspension and an optional removable riser box.

The Proscreen SLG 78 now has the option of Flow Control Wings that lift, rotate and self-centre to improve screening.

Proscreen SLG-68V

The DeSite SLG68V is very portable & makes screening soil easy.

The DeSite SLG68 screens gravel & wet and dry material on-site.
